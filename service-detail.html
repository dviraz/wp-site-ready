Frequently Asked Questions

How often will you post on my social media? ↓

We post daily across all selected platforms (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter) with a total of 30 high-quality posts per month to maintain consistent engagement and reach your target audience effectively.

What platforms do you manage? ↓

We support all major social media platforms including Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn, and TikTok. The specific platforms are chosen based on your target audience and business goals during our strategy session.

Can I review content before it's posted? ↓

Absolutely. We provide a comprehensive content calendar for your review and approval at the beginning of each month. You'll have 48 hours to review and request changes to ensure everything aligns with your brand voice.

What if I need to pause or cancel? ↓

Our services are month-to-month with no long-term contracts. You can pause or cancel anytime with 30 days' notice. We'll provide a smooth transition and hand over all created assets.
